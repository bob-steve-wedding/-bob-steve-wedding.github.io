Our Story

Stephen and Tracey met during their very first week at Drexel University during Freshman Orientation Week in 2011. Tracey was visiting a friend from high school, whose room was on the same floor as Stephen's. A month later, after a loan of laundry detergent, a floor movie night, and a visit to the Academy of Natural Sciences with several of Stephen's floormates, Tracey and Stephen had become fast friends. They also quickly realized that they had grown up in neighboring school districts and participated in a number of activities growing up at the same times and locations, and had probably crossed paths numerous times in the past without ever once meeting!

The two remained close friends for some time before eventually realizing they were probably dating and thus relabeled their relationship. In the time since then, they have since moved to Maryland with their two cats, one of which played a significant role in Stephen's proposal to an oblivious Tracey (who took nearly a full 24 hours to notice) in November 2022.

Tea Ceremony TBD

This is the Chinese tea ceremony, after which the bride and groom were traditionally considered to be married by both families. In this wedding tradition, the bride and groom pay their respects to their parents, new in-laws, and family elders by serving them tea. The ceremony symbolizes the unification of both families and allows the couple the opportunity to honor those who helped raise them. The honorees often bestow blessings and offer the new couple well wishes in their new life together, words of wisdom, or gifts.

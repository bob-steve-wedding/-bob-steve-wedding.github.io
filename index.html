Our Story

Stephen and Tracey met at Drexel University during Freshman Orientation Week in 2011. A month later, after a loan of laundry detergent, a floor movie night, and a visit to the Academy of Natural Sciences with several of Stephen's floormates, Tracey and Stephen had become fast friends. They also quickly realized that they had grown up in neighboring school districts and had, growing up, simultaneously participated in a number of activities during which they likely crossed paths numerous times without ever once meeting!

The two remained close friends for some time before eventually realizing they were probably dating and thus relabeled their relationship. In the time since then, they have moved to Maryland with their two cats, one of which played a significant role in Stephen's proposal to an oblivious Tracey (who took nearly a full 24 hours to notice) in November 2022.

Tea Ceremony 5 PM - 6 PM

In this wedding tradition, the bride and groom pay their respects to their parents, new in-laws, and family elders by serving them tea. The ceremony symbolizes the unification of both families. The honorees often bestow blessings and offer the new couple well wishes in their new life together, words of wisdom, or gifts.

FAQ

Will there be parking available at the wedding venues?

There will be a complimentary shuttle service between the venues and hotels. For those of you that are local, both venues have parking available.
Appleford's disability parking is available at the right fork along the drive, whereas general guest parking is available along the left fork.

Will there be an open bar?

Yes, so please drink and drive responsibly, or plan to take advantage of the hotel room blocks and shuttle service.

Do you have a registry? Should we bring/send wedding gifts?

Your presence at our wedding is the best present you can bring us, and we kindly request no gifts. We will, of course, appreciate cards and will have a card box and guest books at both venues set up.

What should I do in case of illness?

While we would love for you to attend our wedding, we request that if you are feeling unwell, please stay home and take care of yourself.

Will you be live streaming the event?

Unfortunately, we will not the live streaming the events.

Where will wedding updates be posted?

Please check this website for any updates as needed. If you confirm your contact information in your RSVP, we will also send out emails to those whose email contact information we have on file.
